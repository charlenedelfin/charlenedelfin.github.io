Store

There was a time when being a "Zazzler" was a thing in my world, and I created my store only to be part of that crowd. In the beginning, the name of my store was my old Zazzle username, charlenedelfindesigns. (Pun intended. The mischievous person in me thought it would be hilarious to suggest that Charlene Delfin, whom most people described as sweet, actually had "designs".)

It became The Gift of Colors in 2020 because it was a gift shop that had colorful items. These days, the only purpose of the store is to showcase my work with vector designs.
